Our Appointment & Ward Process

At City Care Hospital, we ensure a smooth and transparent process for booking appointments, detecting wards, and assigning doctors. The following steps explain how your healthcare journey flows within our hospital system.

1️⃣ Ward Detection

Our system automatically identifies the required ward and department for your problem mentioned .

2️⃣ Select Appointment Date

Choose from available date slots according to your convenience.

3️⃣ Payment & Wallet Options

Pay securely via med-coins & manage your hospital wallet balance.

4️⃣ OP Sheet Generation

Once booked, an OP sheet is generated and stored in your profile for future reference.

5️⃣ Booking Approval

Your booking request is verified and approved by the hospital management.

6️⃣ Doctor Assignment

Finally, a specialized doctor is assigned by hospital management to handle your case for personalized care.
