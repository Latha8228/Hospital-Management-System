City Hospital - Departments
Orthopedic Department ▶
Specializes in bone and joint problems: fracture management, joint replacement, spinal deformity correction, arthritis care, and sports medicine.
Neurology Department ▶
Focuses on brain, spinal cord, and nerve disorders. Includes stroke management, epilepsy care, migraine treatment, Parkinson's therapy, dementia clinics, and neuro-rehabilitation.
Cardiology Department ▶
Provides heart care: ECG, echocardiography, angioplasty, bypass surgery, treatment of hypertension, arrhythmias, congenital heart conditions, and advanced cardiac interventions.
ENT Department ▶
Treats ear, nose, throat, head, and neck disorders. Services include sinus surgery, tonsillectomy, hearing loss treatment, cochlear implants, balance disorder management, and allergy care.
Dermatology Department ▶
Manages skin, hair, and nail issues: acne, eczema, psoriasis, cosmetic dermatology, anti-aging therapy, hair loss, laser procedures, and skin cancer screening.
Gynecology & Obstetrics ▶
Covers women's healthcare: pregnancy care, infertility solutions, menstrual disorder management, gynecological surgeries, preventive screenings, and maternity services.
Oncology Department ▶
Provides cancer care: chemotherapy, radiation therapy, surgical oncology, immunotherapy, bone marrow transplants, and palliative support.
Nephrology Department ▶
Specializes in kidney health: dialysis, kidney transplant programs, treatment of kidney stones, chronic kidney disease management, and electrolyte imbalance correction.
